FAQ

Questions & answers

Accomplished is a native macOS app. An iOS version is coming soon.

Yes. Daily, weekly, and standup summaries are generated entirely on-device using Apple's built-in foundation models — your data never leaves your Mac. Optional reports (like resume bullets and performance reviews) use a remote AI service to produce more detailed output, but your data is not stored by the service.

Accomplished doesn't store any of your data on its own servers. All of your accomplishments and summaries are stored via iCloud, synced privately through your Apple account. Optional reports send data to a remote AI for processing, but nothing is stored by the service.
